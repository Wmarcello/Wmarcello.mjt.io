About Us

MEGA JAYA TEKINDO

"Innovating Industrial Reliability Through Engineering Excellence"

Since 2015, PT Mega Jaya Tekindo has been a trusted provider of high-performance pump systems, custom cooling solutions, and fire protection systems. We deliver end-to-end industrial engineering services tailored to meet the evolving needs of commercial and industrial facilities across Indonesia.

Your Trusted Partner in Industrial Solutions
PT Mega Jaya Tekindo is a leading provider of industrial equipment and engineering solutions, specializing in:

  • Pump System:
    Supply, installation, and maintenance of centrifugal, multistage, and specialty pumps for HVAC, water treatment, and industrial processes.

  • Chiller & Cooling Systems:
    Customized chiller solutions for commercial and industrial applications.

  • Mechanical & Electrical Services:
    Comprehensive support for machinery, piping, and automation systems.

  • Fire Protection & Diesel Solutions:
    Hydrant Pumps NFPA 20/SNI-compliant electric & diesel fire pumps (up to 5000 GPM, 100+ meter head), jockey pumps, and packaged systems with MTU/Cummins/Perkins engines.and Industrial Systems Custom diesel-driven packages (fire pumps, gensets) for plants, refineries, and infrastructure.
