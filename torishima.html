Torishima CEN Pump Series

Product Description

The Torishima CEN Series centrifugal pumps are designed for heavy-duty industrial applications requiring high efficiency and reliability. These pumps feature:

  • Robust cast iron construction for durability
  • Advanced impeller design for maximum efficiency
  • Mechanical seal options for various applications
  • Wide range of sizes and configurations
  • Compliant with international standards

Capacity Up to 620 m³/h /(170 L/s)
Head Up to 100m
Power 0.75-132 kW
Max. Pressure 10 - 20 bar
Temperature Range -10°C to +120°C

Typical Applications

  • Industrial water supply
  • Cooling water circulation
  • Boiler feed systems
  • Chemical processing
  • HVAC systems
  • General industrial pumping
