Ebara FSA Pump Series

Product Description

The Ebara FSA Series centrifugal pumps are built for efficient fluid transfer and high reliability in industrial and commercial applications.

  • Close-coupled single-stage pump
  • Cast iron casing with stainless steel shaft
  • Back pull-out design for easy maintenance
  • High hydraulic efficiency with closed impeller
  • Compliant with ISO 2858 standards

Technical Specifications

Model FSA
Capacity Up to 1500 m³/h
Head Up to 150m
Power Up to 225 kW
Max. Pressure 16 bar
Temperature Range -10°C to +120°C

Typical Applications

  • Water supply systems
  • Industrial circulation systems
  • Cooling towers
  • Fire-fighting systems
  • Irrigation and agriculture
  • Booster systems

Ebara GS Pump Series

Product Description

The Ebara GS Series is a versatile, end-suction centrifugal pump ideal for clean water applications across industrial and commercial use cases. It offers exceptional hydraulic performance and easy maintenance.

Ebara EVMSG Pump Series

Product Description

The Ebara EVMSG Series is a high-efficiency vertical multistage centrifugal pump, engineered for clean water applications where compact size and high performance are critical.

  • Vertical, close-coupled multistage pump
  • All wetted parts in stainless steel AISI 304 or AISI 316
  • Ideal for boosting systems and pressure units
  • High efficiency and low noise operation
  • Available in various stages for different head requirements

Technical Specifications

Model EVMSG
Capacity Up to 20 m³/h
Head Up to 250m
Power 0.37 kW to 5.5 kW
Max. Pressure 25 bar
Temperature Range -10°C to +90°C

Typical Applications

  • Booster sets
  • RO systems
  • Pressure boosting
  • Water treatment plants
  • Cooling towers
  • Industrial washing
